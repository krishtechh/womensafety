🔍 What is this Project?

This is a voice-activated women safety system that sends your live location via WhatsApp and starts emergency calls — all triggered by just saying “Help [Your Name]”. It also sends an SOS alert to the nearest police station.

⚙️ How It Works

  • 🔐 Log in and save 3 emergency contacts.
  • 🎙️ The system listens for your SOS phrase: “Help [Your Name]”.
  • 📍 Your live location is shared via WhatsApp with all contacts.
  • 📞 It starts calling the contacts repeatedly until someone answers.
  • 🚨 Sends an SOS alert to nearby police with location data.

💡 Why Use It?

  • ✔ Hands-free emergency activation
  • ✔ Real-time live location sharing
  • ✔ Auto-calling feature ensures someone responds
  • ✔ Direct police alert integration

🌟 What Makes It Unique?

Unlike most safety apps that require manual input, this system works **completely hands-free** using voice commands, and continues to call your emergency contacts until someone answers — maximizing your chances of getting immediate help. Also the first Project to have all the three features Send Location, Call the Person and Send a SOS to Police.

🔐 Privacy & Security

All your data, including contacts and voice samples, are stored securely. The system only activates with your chosen phrase and never shares data without your explicit trigger.

❓ FAQs

  • Q: Do I need internet for it to work? A: Yes, internet is required for WhatsApp messaging and location sharing.
  • Q: Can I update my emergency contacts? A: Yes, you can change them anytime from your dashboard.
  • Q: Is the voice command customizable? A: Currently, it uses a fixed format: “Help [Your Name]” for accuracy.
